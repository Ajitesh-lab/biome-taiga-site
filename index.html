Security & Privacy.

Clear boundaries for local autonomy.

Your conversations, memory, and API keys live on your machine. Biome asks before sensitive local actions.

Local First, Always

Conversations, memory, and API keys stay on your machine. AI calls go directly from your device to your chosen provider. We only store your email address.

SSRF Hardened

All outbound requests are validated against host allowlists and private-IP DNS checks. The gateway cannot be tricked into fetching from internal hosts.

Exec Approval Required

Every command the AI wants to run requires your explicit approval. Approval timeouts result in a hard block — no silent fallback execution path.

Trust Isolation

External content — webhooks, wake events, runtime notifications — is quarantined as untrusted before entering the AI's context.

Clean Session History

Internal tool data and system metadata are stripped from all user-facing views. What you see in chat is exactly what the model intended.

Rate Limited Backend

Per-IP rate limits on every endpoint. OTP codes expire in 10 minutes and cannot be reused. Everything else runs entirely on your machine.

Pro unlocks Browser Control through the local browser relay and lightweight Chrome extension, AI Chat Piggyback, Zero-Knowledge privacy mode, and priority support.

Almost everything stays on your machine. Conversations, memory, and settings are stored locally by the desktop app — never on our servers. API calls go directly from your device to whichever AI provider you choose. We only keep account, billing, and consent records needed to run the service.

Browser Control is a Pro feature that lets your agent open URLs, click elements, fill forms, and read page content in Chrome through Biome's local browser relay and lightweight Chrome extension. It's not required for basic web search — that works without Chrome. Browser Control unlocks deeper automation like reading paywalled content, filling forms, and interacting with web apps.
